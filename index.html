The Elite Mercenaries

Barney Ross leads the 'Expendables', a band of highly skilled mercenaries including knife enthusiast Lee Christmas, martial arts expert Yin Yang, heavy weapons specialist Hale Caesar, demolitionist Toll Road and loose-cannon sniper Gunner Jensen. They carry all sorts of missions, ranging from assassination to rescue. Their missions are nothing short of dangerous as in some instances they lose a member.
